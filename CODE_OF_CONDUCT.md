# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

The Advertising Hub exists to make advertising automation accessible to everyone — from solo media buyers to enterprise teams. We're committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive environment for all contributors regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Encouraged behavior:**

- Sharing hard-won platform knowledge that helps others avoid expensive mistakes
- Providing constructive feedback on agents, patterns, and MCP servers
- Helping newcomers understand advertising APIs and tooling
- Crediting sources and upstream projects

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Sharing API keys, tokens, or credentials (even "test" ones)
- Promoting specific agencies, tools, or services disguised as contributions
- Dismissing questions because they seem "basic" — everyone starts somewhere
- Harassing, trolling, or personally attacking other contributors

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior can be reported to john@itallstartedwithaidea.com. All reports will be reviewed and acted upon promptly.
