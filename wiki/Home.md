## What Makes This Different

Most advertising repos are platform-specific SDK libraries. The Advertising Hub is the **horizontal layer** that connects them:

1. **Curated** — Only advertising-relevant repos from each platform (no mobile SDKs, no deprecated SOAP libraries)
2. **Connected** — Shared `core/` package with unified auth, models, and error handling
3. **AI-Native** — Agents and MCP servers designed for AI-powered advertising automation
4. **Practitioner-Written** — Patterns and gotchas from 15+ years of enterprise ad management
