# Platform Index

All 14 advertising platforms in the hub, organized by category.

## Search Advertising
- [[Google Ads]] — The largest search platform. ⚡ Live MCP server, 4 agents, full patterns.
- [[Microsoft Ads]] — Bing search + audience network. Import-from-Google + unique features.

## Social Advertising
- [[Meta Ads]] — Facebook + Instagram. Largest social ad platform.
- [[LinkedIn Ads]] — B2B social. Job title/company/seniority targeting.
- [[Pinterest Ads]] — Visual commerce. Planning intent → purchase.
- [[Reddit Ads]] — Community-based. Subreddit and interest targeting.

## Commerce & Marketplace
- [[Amazon Ads]] — Sponsored Products/Brands/Display + DSP. Where search meets purchase.

## Programmatic & Display
- [[The Trade Desk]] — Open internet DSP. Kokai AI, UID2, CTV.
- [[Criteo]] — Commerce media. Dynamic retargeting.
- [[AdRoll]] — SMB cross-channel retargeting.

## Account-Based Marketing
- [[Demandbase]] — ABM platform. Intent signals, account targeting.

## Audio
- [[Spotify Ads]] — Audio and podcast advertising.

## Q&A / Intent
- [[Quora Ads]] — Question and topic targeting.
