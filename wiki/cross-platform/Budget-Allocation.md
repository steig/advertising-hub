# Cross-Platform Budget Allocation

## The Diminishing Returns Framework

Every platform has a point where additional spend yields declining incremental results. The optimal allocation puts each platform at or just below its efficiency threshold.

## Allocation by Funnel Stage

| Funnel Stage | Platforms | Typical % of Budget |
|-------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Brand Search | Google, Microsoft | 10-15% |
| Non-Brand Search | Google, Microsoft, Amazon | 25-35% |
| Shopping / Commerce | Google, Amazon, Pinterest | 15-25% |
| Social Prospecting | Meta, LinkedIn, Pinterest | 15-25% |
| Retargeting | Meta, Google, Criteo | 10-15% |
| Programmatic | TTD, DV360 | 5-15% |
| ABM | Demandbase, LinkedIn | 5-10% |

## Reallocation Triggers

- Platform CPA exceeds target by >20% for 2+ weeks → reduce
- Platform has >20% impression share headroom with stable CPA → increase
- Seasonal patterns shift demand (e.g., B2B quiets Dec, ecom peaks Nov)
- New platform test shows promising early results → graduate from test budget

## Budget Sizing for New Platforms

When adding a new platform, start with a test budget:
- **Minimum viable test**: 3x your target CPA × 30 days
- **Example**: $50 CPA target → $4,500/month test budget minimum
- **Duration**: 6-8 weeks for statistical significance
